# 🌱 Sustainable Squad

## Overview

Sustainable Squad is a student-led environmental movement established to increase youth participation in sustainability through education, campaigns, leadership, and digital engagement.

---

## Mission

To inspire students to become environmentally responsible leaders through collaborative sustainability initiatives.

---

## Activities

- School campaigns
- Social media awareness
- Sustainability workshops
- Environmental competitions
- Community engagement

---

## Our Role

- Founders
- Student Leaders
- Campaign Coordinators
- Public Speakers

---

## Future Scope

Develop Sustainable Squad into an international youth sustainability network using technology, AI, and collaborative environmental action.
