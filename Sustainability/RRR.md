# ♻ RRR (Reduce • Reuse • Recycle)

## Overview

RRR is a student-led environmental initiative founded to encourage responsible resource management and sustainable living through education, awareness, and community participation.

The initiative promotes practical environmental actions that reduce waste while encouraging students and communities to adopt sustainable habits.

---

## Objectives

- Promote waste reduction
- Encourage recycling
- Support responsible consumption
- Increase environmental awareness
- Inspire community participation

---

## Activities

- Recycling campaigns
- Environmental awareness programs
- School initiatives
- Community outreach
- Sustainability education

---

## Impact

RRR has encouraged students and community members to participate in environmental initiatives while fostering long-term awareness of sustainable practices.

---

## Future Vision

Expand RRR into a technology-supported environmental platform integrating Artificial Intelligence, citizen science, and community-driven sustainability initiatives.
