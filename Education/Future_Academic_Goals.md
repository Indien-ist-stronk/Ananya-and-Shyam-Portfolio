# Future Academic Goals

Our long-term educational goals include pursuing higher studies in fields that combine technology, sustainability, and scientific research.

Areas we hope to specialize in include:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Sustainable AI
- Engineering
- Environmental Science
- Climate Technology

We aspire to contribute to interdisciplinary research while developing practical solutions that address global environmental and societal challenges.

In the future, we hope to collaborate with universities, research laboratories, government organizations, and industry partners to transform innovative ideas into real-world impact.
