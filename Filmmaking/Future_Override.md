## Synopsis

Future Override explores a future where Artificial Intelligence governs society.

When humanity's future is placed at risk, Maya receives a mysterious opportunity to travel back in time and prevent the creation of an uncontrolled AI system.

The film explores the importance of responsible innovation, ethical Artificial Intelligence, environmental sustainability, and the choices that shape humanity's future.
