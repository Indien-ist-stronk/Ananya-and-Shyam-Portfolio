# Career Vision

Our long-term ambition is to become interdisciplinary researchers working at the intersection of:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Sustainable AI
* Engineering
* Environmental Science
* Healthcare Innovation
* Climate Technology

We hope to develop intelligent technologies that support sustainable development while improving people's quality of life.

Beyond research, we aspire to communicate scientific ideas through filmmaking, creative media, and public engagement.

We believe future innovation should combine:

* Science
* Technology
* Creativity
* Ethics
* Sustainability

to solve humanity's most pressing challenges.
